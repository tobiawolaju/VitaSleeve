Developing technology for humanity.

Lab7 is dedicated to creating simple, open-source, and affordable technology that aligns with the UN's Sustainable Development Goals. All our designs, software, and research are open-source for DIY makers.

What is Lab7?

Lab7 is a research initiative focused on simple, open-source, and affordable technology for sustainable development. We aim to empower communities by providing accessible tools and knowledge. The project is open-source: view schematics, code, and 3D-printable parts.

Core Focus Areas

  • Affordable Educational Tools: Low-cost kits for learning and innovation. 3D-printable parts
  • Open-Source Software: Freely available code for humanitarian applications. Control software
  • Sustainable Agriculture Tech: Simple sensors and tools for small-scale farming. Circuit schematics
  • Community Health Solutions: Basic diagnostic tools and health monitoring systems.
  • Renewable Energy Systems: DIY guides for small-scale solar and wind power.
  • Water Purification Methods: Simple, effective, and low-cost water treatment solutions.

The Mission

Our mission is to foster a global community of innovators dedicated to solving real-world problems. We believe in the power of open-source collaboration to create a more sustainable and equitable future for all. Learn more about building your own: Open-source resources.
