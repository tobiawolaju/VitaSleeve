Replacing hospitals, not humans.

Our mission is to provide continuous, intelligent, and autonomous care to anyone, anywhere.

The Mission

We believe that world-class healthcare shouldn't be confined to a building. VitaSleeve is an open-source platform designed to be a wearable, autonomous extension of a person's life, providing everything from mobility assistance to critical life support in a single, integrated system.

How It Works

VitaSleeve integrates three core systems:

  • Neural Link: A non-invasive interface reads and translates neural signals into seamless prosthetic motion and system commands.
  • Medical Systems: A suite of micro-sensors and automated dispensers monitor vitals and administer care under AI supervision.
  • Prosthetics: Lightweight, high-torque limbs and structural supports offer full-body mobility and strength.

Safety & Open-Source Vision

Safety is our primary directive. The core operating system is fully open-source, allowing for community-driven audits and improvements. All medical functions operate on a secure, isolated subsystem with multiple redundancies. We are committed to a future where care technology is transparent, accessible, and community-governed.
