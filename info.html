VitaSleeve is an integrated robotic life-support system designed to deliver continuous medical care, mobility, and autonomy to anyone, anywhere.

What is VitaSleeve?

VitaSleeve is a wearable, full-body exosuit with onboard medical infrastructure, prosthetic robotics, and autonomous humanoid assistance. It is built for people with chronic conditions, mobility challenges, disabilities, injuries, or aging-related weakness.

Core Systems

  • Robotic Exosuit: Full-body strength amplification, balance support, and prosthetic motion.
  • Neural Interface: Non-invasive control through neural decoding, EMG fusion, voice and gesture inputs.
  • Medical Infrastructure: Vitals monitoring, insulin delivery, dialysis-light filtration, micro heart-lung support.
  • Emergency Suite: Auto bleeding control, infection detection, stabilization tools, distress beaconing.
  • Surgical & Imaging: Micro surgical arm, ultrasound, light-MRI, X-ray micro-imaging, AI diagnostics.
  • Autonomous Humanoid Mode: Can operate as a standalone robot assistant for mobility, lifting, and home tasks.

The Mission

Healthcare should not depend on buildings, distance, or luck. VitaSleeve brings continuous care directly to the person who needs it, providing real-time support, mobility, diagnostics, and emergency stabilizing tools. Our mission is simple: protect life, restore independence, and empower every human body.
